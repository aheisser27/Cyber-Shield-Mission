🛡️ Level 1: The Apprentice

Situation Report:

Every website you visit is built on raw code. The "Hunter" often leaves footprints in this code, assuming regular users will never look behind the curtain. As a Defender, your first skill is learning how to see the invisible.

Your Objective

  • Find the hidden "Flag" left by the Hunter.
  • It looks like this: FLAG{SOME_TEXT_HERE}.

Action Steps

1. Right-click anywhere on the white space of this page.

2. Select "View Page Source" from the menu.

3. Look for the green text (these are called HTML Comments). Find the flag and report it!
