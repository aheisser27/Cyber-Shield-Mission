⚠️ Level 2: Log Analysis

Situation Report:

You have intercepted a live server log. Hunters know that security teams are busy, so they hide their attacks inside thousands of lines of normal "digital chatter," hoping you'll get tired of reading.

Your Objective

The Hunter tagged their file with the word BOUNTY. You must find it in the log below.

Action Steps

Scrolling will take too long. Work smarter, not harder.

Use your keyboard: Press Ctrl + F (or Cmd + F on a Mac) to open the search tool. Type the word BOUNTY to instantly jump to the hidden flag.
