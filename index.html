🛡️ The Cyber Shield Mission Hub

Welcome, Defenders. Your objective is to find the hidden "Flags" in each of the targets below. A flag looks like this: FLAG{EXAMPLE_TEXT}. Start at Level 1 and work your way up. If you get stuck, click the "Need a hint?" dropdown.

Target 1: The Apprentice (Easy)

The "Shield" begins with seeing what others miss. Can you translate the web page into its raw code?

Need a hint?

Right-click anywhere on the white background of the target page and click 'View Page Source'. Look for the green text!

Target 2: The Analyst (Medium)

Hunters hide their tracks in massive amounts of data. Find the "BOUNTY" in the noise.

Need a hint?

You are scrolling too fast. Use the keyboard shortcut Ctrl + F and type the word BOUNTY.

Target 3: The Specialist (Hard)

The Hunter scrambled the data using Base64. You will need external tools to decode it.

Need a hint?

Highlight the scrambled text, copy it, and open a new tab. Google 'Base64 Decoder' or 'CyberChef', paste the text in, and decode it.

Target 4: The Elite (Extra Hard)

The HTML is clean. You need to dig deeper into the browser's developer tools.

Need a hint?

Hit the F12 key on your keyboard, then look for the tab that says 'Console' at the top of that new menu.
